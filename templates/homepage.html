Terminology

Steganography

The practice of concealing a message within another medium, such as an image or audio file, to prevent detection.

PKI

A system using public and private keys to secure communications and manage digital certificates for identity verification.

Encryption

The process of transforming plaintext into ciphertext using a key, making it unreadable without authorization.
